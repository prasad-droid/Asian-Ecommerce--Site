Refund policy

If you are not satisfied with your order, we want to make it right! Please follow the instructions below so we can process your request as quickly as possible.

Asian will gladly accept returns for merchandise purchased through asianfootwears.com that meet the following conditions:

Merchandise must be returned in its original unworn condition and received WITHIN 7 DAYS OF DELIVERY DATE. Orders returned beyond this time period will not be accepted. Asian Shoes reserves the right to refuse worn or damaged merchandise.

Please ensure that the tag attached to the product is safely in place. Pack the merchandise in its original packaging or appropriate carton.

A refund would take place within 2-3 business days post receipt of the products back at our warehouse. Careful monitoring of the returned products takes place to ensure that the shoes are unworn and intact. Subject to discretion is with "Asian Footwears Pvt. Ltd."
